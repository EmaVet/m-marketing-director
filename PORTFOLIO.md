## Pipeline & Revenue

### PayTo University — GoCardless
**$1M+ pipeline generated for a product that didn't exist yet**

PayTo was a new bank payments standard — complex, technical, and still in development. I conceived and launched an education-first campaign that positioned GoCardless as the category authority before competitors had a product story. The pipeline it generated came from buyers who trusted us before the product launched.

*What it took:* A content strategy built around education not features. Deep collaboration with product and sales to get the technical framing right. Patience with a long sales cycle.

---

### Cold Outbound Email Program — GoCardless
**$2M+ pipeline — later scaled as a global model**

ANZ's first structured cold email program. Built from scratch: targeting, copy, sequencing, measurement. Generated $2M+ in pipeline and was adopted by other regions as a repeatable playbook.

*What it took:* Rigorous audience segmentation, tight collaboration with SDR, and willingness to test and iterate on messaging until it worked.

---

### Direct Mail & Strategic Gifting — GoCardless
**10x ROI — adopted globally as ABM best practice**

In a market flooded with digital, physical stood out. A direct mail and gifting program targeting high-value accounts delivered 10x ROI and became the ABM benchmark replicated across global markets.

*What it took:* Creative risk-taking, precise targeting, and a clear feedback loop with sales to measure what actually moved opportunities.

### Sage Integration Partnership — Employment Hero
**$1M+ pipeline in 6 months**

Built a global co-marketing framework with Sage as the first major integration partnership launch. Generated $1M+ in pipeline within six months and established the repeatable model used for subsequent ecosystem partnerships.

*What it took:* A new global partner GTM framework, alignment across five regions, and tight collaboration between partner, product, and sales teams.
